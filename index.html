ABOUT

The department of Electronics and communication Engineering was established in the year 1994 with the aim of creating young and budding engineers with necessary technical knowledge and professional skills. We aspire to impart to our students the value of communication and realization that they can make a difference. to train the students to be employed in multi-disciplinary areas including allied academic disciplines.The department also offers PG programme - M.E Applied Electronics to the students. It emphasizes the design of hardware systems as well as communication systems.

The purpose of a symposium is for industry experts to highlight their recent discoveries and the latest in research developments in a given field of study. Unlike a conference or seminar, a symposium usually involved the presentation of original research by the researchers themselves
